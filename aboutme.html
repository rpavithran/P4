About Me


My name is Rhea Pavithran, and I'm a senior studying at NJIT, majoring in Business Information Systems.


I've been dancing since I was 4 years old, starting with ballet and tap. I later moved on to Indian classical dance a year later in order to get in touch with my roots, and I've been practicing ever since. Now, I've done 3 types of classical styles, as well as Bollywood, South Indian dance, and Bhangra.


I teach my own dance classes around the NJ/NY area because I think it's extremely important to have fun while getting in shape, and Indian dance is the perfect way for me to share my culture and have fun!
